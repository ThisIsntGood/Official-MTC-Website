The Basic Wizards

  • Newbie Wizard (Attacks you if you get too close)
  • Flying Wizard (Once it spots the player, it will quickly dart towards the sky, followed by quick flight, homing in on the player)
  • Flame Wizard (Shoots fireballs at a straight line)
  • Electro Wizard (Shoots lighting bolts that paralyze the player, dealing little damage over time)
  • Aqua Wizard (Always by a pond, and uses it's magic to pull out some water, having a different chance to pull out different kinds of dangerous fish, like Pufferfish for poison or Electric Eels for a stun)
  • Stone Wizard (Summons Stalagmites from the ground as well can turn into stone to increase it's health tenfold)

    • The Advanced Wizard

  • Experinced Wizard (Shoots moderately slow projectiles at the player)
  • Jetpack Wizard (Shoots lazers that apply a burn affect for 3-5 seconds)
  • Molten Wizard (Summons small volcanoes that shoot out small meteors/fireballs, leaving a small area of flame, damaging anyone that comes across it)
  • Storm Wizard (Summons a lighting cloud that slowly follows the player around the area)
  • Ice Wizard (Rains down Icicles that damages everyone but itself and said Icicles will be embeded into the ground)
  • Brick Wizard (Throws bricks at the player dealing massive damage, having a chance to throw a tungsten cube, instakilling the player)

    • The Fortune Wizards

  • Copper Wizard (Drops more money than usual)
  • Silver Wizard (Drops even more money as well as shoots spikes at the player)
  • Gold Wizard (Drops a ton of money as well as sprints at a fast pace but gets easily exausted)
  • Emerald Wizard (Heals other wizards as it runs away)

    • Miscellaneous Wizards

  • Mini Wizard (Latches onto the player dealing unoticeable damage, but deals more damage the more there are)
  • Gargantuant Wizard (Charges at the player, defeating any wizards in the way as well as breaks any obstacles in the way unless it's spikes. It's weak point is it's head.)
  • Jester Wizard (Pulls out a comically large toy hammer that does little damage, but will reverse your left and right movement controls for a short period of time. Can also pull out balloons that, once touched, will shoot out compressed air with spikes towards the wizard/player that touched it. There are NO attack patterns at all)

  • The Boss Wizards

  • Slime Wizard (Starts off large but splits up in 2 when defeated)
  • Skelly Wizard (Summons "Skellies" that come back from death until the Skelly Wizard is defeated)
  • Shadow and Light Wizards (The Shadow Wizard is the ambusher of the duo, decends into a shadow that moves around the floor, and once he appears out of hiding, he will do a powerful projectile of dark matter. The Light Wizard is the support, locking onto the player, then shooting a projectile that blinds the player's screen on impact, allowing for the Shadow Wizard to attack.)
