MTC

ThisIsntGood

ThisIsntGood, also called Malik Dorleans, is a moderately skilled digital artist and/or animator who has been drawing since early December of 2023. He is the first member of MTC.

He has started his career of drawing by making some sketches in his algebra notebook, sketches of an overly simply designed character that would represent him, an OC if you will, who eventually was named Disim. Since then he has been making new characters, representing a different part of him.

He had no idea what to do with these characters until the second half of his school year, where he met MrScriptDev and CocoCrawl. He found out that MrScriptDev was pretty great at coding, and that CocoCrawl was also great at coding, thus they decided to make a game, a 2D platformer in the land of Squitanica, taking place in ThisIsntGood's mind.

In the game you would play as a character named Disim, who is traversing island to island to find his friends, who have each been captured by wizards, who are nothing but diabolically evil.

MrScriptDev

MrScriptDev, otherwise known as Davis Hawkins, is a professional coder since 2022. He is the second member of MTC.

He has made projects by making designs on scratch and putting them on coding applications such as Godot or Visual Studio Code.

His projects would consist of...

  • Zombos, a game where you play as a survivor and there are a TON of zombies, a.k.a Zombos. You have to either run or fight against the Zombos with the tools necessary.
  • Crazy Bus, a game where you play as a bus driver that has to pick up kids and drop them off to their school, and you can customize your bus too!
  • Fire-Copter, a game where you have to control a helicopter that sprays water in order to destroy the fires occupying the buildings.

CocoCrawl

CocoCrawl, also known as Coco Byars, is masterly skilled at music composing, which he has been doing within the time range of 2020-2021. He is the third member of MTC.

Memeulus

Memeulus, A.K.A Jacob Hamel, is a really skilled 3D artist who has been doing it since 2023. He is the fourth member of MTC.

He uses Blender to make 3D versions of any funny animations that ThisIsntGood has made.
